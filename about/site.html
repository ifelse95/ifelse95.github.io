about the site

ifelse95's house was established around november 12th, 2020. it began firstly as a zonelets site on neocities.

it began as a blog to talk about my thoughts, share ideas, document development of my projects, and more. then i started to add things like galleries and other pages. eventually, i realized that the zonelets format wasn't going to work for a fully fledged site, so i began to learn html and css properly and create a whole site.

ifelse95's house is a personal website for sharing art, thoughts, collections, things we like, and more. it does not cater to anyone in specific, except to anyone who would like a glimpse into our soul. it is unapologetically loud, cringey, and carefree.

this is our little corner of the web and we do as we please.

ifelse95's house was hosted on our partner system's server, but it recently died. it briefly returned to neocities, before settling on github. the zonelets blog remains as a piece of this site, and is now called ifelse95's journal.
