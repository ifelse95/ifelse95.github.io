Koikatsu Cards

Here is the place for my cards. Please treat them nicely, especially my OCs. Pretty please? It's all I ask. I can take down these cards at any time.

To download, click on the image, and you will be taken to the download site. Right click the card and choose "Save Image as...". Then save the card to your UserData/chara/female or /male directories.

The cards will be labeled with an (F) or and (M) to indicate which directory they belong in (this does not refer to the character's actual gender, however).
