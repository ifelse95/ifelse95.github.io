GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

Using this template is quite easy.

  1. Select your preferred version. The ToyHouse version has its own instructions on how to set up, and the .docx version can be downloaded to your device and opened in your choice of word processing software.
  2. After opening/setting up, simply just replace any placeholder text and fill in your own information!
