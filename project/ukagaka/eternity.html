ETERNITY

A ghost/ukagaka for SSP

Eternity is a runaway from a strange dimension of shape-shifters. Having wandered around different universes and dimensions, they have wound up on your computer! You can't help but to wonder... is your desktop an entire dimension of it's own? Regardless, it looks like they're here to stay.

Eternity is an Ukagaka (伺か, or ghost) developed using Zichqec's X. Template.









WHAT CAN ETERNITY DO?

Eternity will be story based over function based. You can gain their trust and hear more about their story, as well as ask them questions and give them gifts, if you would like. This is all in addition to the features listed on my Ukagaka Project Page index. In addition addition, Eternity currently also;

  • Has sound effects (SFX) that can be turned off or on
  • Can comment on a couple different programs that you are using (by only looking at the window titles). This can also be turned off or on
  • Can give you your screen resolution.
  • Can give you resources for mental health, if you happen to need it.*
  • More to come...
