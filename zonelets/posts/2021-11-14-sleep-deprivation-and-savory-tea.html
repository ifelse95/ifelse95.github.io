one of my favorite mugs, my maneki-neko mug.

long time no see. i've been through it, but things a looking up a little bit.

it's close to 7 am at the start of writing this. i've been up since 2 pm yesterday, with a nap somewhere in between. yesterday, i went to the asian market.

i've always loved asian markets. not only for the nice snacks, but also because there's so much to see. this particular one is the coolest i've been to so far. i actually got my maneki-neko mug there, too, a year or two ago.

i got some pretty good stuff. matcha kit kats, matcha pocky, matcha (did you know i like matcha?) and taro flavored bubble tea in cans... as well as white peach calpico and some aloe juice, as well as some peach gummies and a fidget popper toy (a 3 pack actually, i gave away the other two). good stuff!

oooh, this stuff was good. better than expected. i had to drink it after dinner, though, for reasons i will discuss in a second.

there is one item i left out though... let me give some context. recently, i have been told that i am a t2 diabetic. it's not really a shock, i have a huge sweet tooth despite knowing that diabetes is very genetic in my family (both sides). basically, i'm a dipshit and im now working to control my sugars. i say this and then buy a bunch of sweets... it's a process.

so, the item... a "blood sugar" tea. found completely by chance. i had got lost in the store and glanced up at a shelf, and there it was. and i'm going to be completely honest, i incredibly doubt that this tea is going to help. i just wanted to humor myself. so, i went ahead and picked it up.

fast forward to me about an hour ago now. i have been up for hours after laying down for bed. i finally throw in the towel and get up to do something about my insomnia. i decided to make some tea, and i remembered the "blood sugar" tea i had bought the day before.

i heat up my water, stick in the tea bag, cover up the cup and scroll through reddit for about 10 minutes. i noticed when i opened up the packet, it smelled... savory. which was interesting, because i had never smelled "savory" tea before. needless to say, i was very curious.

i uncover my cup and take a sip... and... it tasted exactly how it smelled. savory. it was like, drinking a soup...

now, i didn't dislike it! i actually enjoyed it. but it was new. i loved drinking tea as a kid, but kind of fell out of it in favor of coffee and energy drinks when i began highschool. it was only recently i started drinking tea again (with a much broader selection than i had as a kid, too). basically, in all the tea i've drank in my life, this was brand new. and i liked it.

while the intent was to soothe myself to sleep... it actually woke me up and made my focus a bit sharper. weird. maybe i ought to check my sugar soon.

i don't think i'm going to bed any time soon. the sun is rising now, so at best i'll probably get a nap in later. i guess now is a good time to make breakfast and start the day.

i hope everyone is doing okay. i'm not sure who reads this blog, if anyone, but just in general i hope people are okay. i had this banger in my spotify discover weekly, it's stuck in my head now! take care.
